Almond Polaroid Cam

How to Use:
1) Tap "Start Camera" below to go to the camera page.
2) Take a photo by pressing the "Take Photo" button (the countdown is 5 seconds). You can select an aesthetic filter at the bottom of the camera.
3) After the countdown ends, you will be redirected to the photo preview page. You can choose to proceed or retake the photo.
4) Once you choose to proceed, you will be taken to the frame customization page. Follow the available instructions.
