Almond Polaroid Cam

Custom Polaroid • Halftone • Eco Packaging • Spotify Code

Welcome to Almond Polaroid Cam. This is a simple service for creating personalized polaroid prints that blend printed art style with digital features and sustainable materials.

You can choose camera filters, frame colors, and stickers for each print. There is a halftone option with dot sizes from extra small to extra large, and a colorful version is available. A scannable Spotify code can be added to the print for a music touch. Packaging uses recycled paper for the polaroid sheet and 3 mm MDF for small accessories.

Our core values are Comforting, Creating, and Caring. We aim to deliver a pleasant experience, encourage creative choices, and keep sustainability in mind for people and the planet.
